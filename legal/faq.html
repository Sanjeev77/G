Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is Gift On Budget?

Gift On Budget is a curated platform that helps you find perfect gifts organized by budget ranges. We feature products from Amazon across various categories, making it easy to find thoughtful gifts within your price range.

How do you choose the products featured on your site?

Our team carefully selects products based on customer reviews, ratings, popularity, and value for money. We focus on items that offer the best combination of quality and affordability within each budget category.

Are the prices on your site accurate?

Prices are updated regularly, but Amazon prices can change frequently. We recommend checking the current price on Amazon before making a purchase. All purchases are completed through Amazon's secure checkout process.

Shopping & Orders

Do I purchase items directly from Gift On Budget?

No, all purchases are made directly through Amazon. When you click on a product, you'll be redirected to Amazon where you can complete your purchase using Amazon's secure checkout process.

What about shipping and returns?

All shipping, returns, and customer service are handled by Amazon according to their standard policies. If you have Amazon Prime, you'll still get your Prime benefits including free shipping.

Can I track my order through Gift On Budget?

Order tracking is handled through your Amazon account. After making a purchase, you can track your order status in your Amazon account or through Amazon's mobile app.

Affiliate Information

Do you earn money from the products I purchase?

Yes, Gift On Budget participates in the Amazon Associates Program. This means we may earn a small commission if you make a purchase through our links, at no additional cost to you. This helps us maintain and improve our service.

Does the affiliate commission affect the price I pay?

No, the affiliate commission does not increase the price you pay. The price you see on Amazon is the same whether you go directly to Amazon or through our links.

Technical Questions

Why don't some product links work?

Occasionally, products may become unavailable on Amazon or links may need updating. If you encounter a broken link, please try searching for the product name directly on Amazon.

How can I suggest products or improvements?

We're always looking to improve! While we're setting up formal feedback channels, we appreciate any suggestions you might have for new products or site improvements.
